    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What is a Java Full Stack Developer?

    A Java Full Stack Developer is skilled in both frontend and backend technologies, building complete web applications.

    What technologies are taught in this course?

    The course covers Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, Angular, REST APIs, and database management (MySQL/PostgreSQL).

    Are there any prerequisites for this course?

    Basic programming knowledge is recommended, but we provide foundational training for beginners.

    What kind of projects will I work on?

    You will build real-world applications, such as e-commerce platforms, management systems, and social media apps.

    What is the course duration?

    The course typically lasts for 3 to 6 months, depending on your pace and learning style.

    Will I receive a certificate after completion?

    Yes, upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a certification.

    How can I get career support after the course?

    We provide resume building, interview preparation, and job placement assistance to all our students.
